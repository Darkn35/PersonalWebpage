Spiderman


   “With great power comes great responsibility.” -Spiderman

Spider-Man is the alias of Peter Parker, an orphan raised by his Aunt May and Uncle Ben in New York City after his parents Richard and Mary Parker were killed in a plane crash. A bite from a radioactive spider triggers mutations in Peter Parker's body, granting him superpowers.
